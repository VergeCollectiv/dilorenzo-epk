About

Chris' relationship with electronic music started in 2014. Drawn in by the energy of artists like Martin Garrix and DVBBS, what began as a casual interest quickly became a decade long obsession with discovering music, following artists, and understanding the culture that surrounds dance music.

Over the years, that passion evolved into a deep appreciation for the subtle elements that make a great set memorable. The pacing, the track selection, the tension and release, and the ability to shape the atmosphere of a room through sound.

Today, his sound is rooted in minimal house, groovy basslines, rolling percussion, and melodic textures. Influenced by artists such as Mochakk, Cloonee, Franky Rizardo, Prospa, and Ranger Trucco, his sets balance energy and restraint, focusing on creating a natural progression that keeps people moving while maintaining a strong sense of atmosphere. In 2026, Chris began producing music, building on years spent studying dance floors, collecting records, and refining his musical taste.

Community

In 2023, Chris co-founded Verge Collectiv, a Toronto based event brand built around community, wellness, and music.

What started as a passion project among friends has grown into a platform that brings people together through thoughtfully curated experiences, blending wellness programming, unique venues, and house music driven events.

From sauna and cold plunge experiences to rooftop gatherings and community focused nightlife, Verge was built on the belief that meaningful connections happen when the right people are brought together in the right environment.
